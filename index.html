MAIN INTERESTS

My work is mainly concerned with finding what regions are perceptually important in images. More precisely, I investigate computationally efficient approaches for estimating such regions. This problem lies in the intersection of areas such as computer science, psychology and neuroscience, and is useful for applications in multimedia, robot vision, among others.
